Research
Satellite imagery · GIS

Generalizable slum segmentation via remote sensing

Informal settlements lack reliable geospatial datasets, making global poverty mapping difficult. I build datasets and models that generalize across regions without local labels — using Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) to capture regional visual variation and Test-Time Adaptation (TTA) for zero-shot transfer to unseen geographies.

LLM agents · ongoing

LLM corruption via bribery game theory

LLMs are increasingly deployed in public-facing roles as neutral arbiters, yet can behave self-interestedly under certain conditions. I use a bribery game framework — where rational agents have no incentive to deviate — to isolate when and why models engage in corrupt behavior. The framework extends to LLM–human interactions in governance-like settings.
